Photography website planning site

Designing a Photography Website

The purpose of this website is to enable people living in mainland China to access Helen's grandparents photography website since they only have a Facebook page that is rarely updated and is inaccessible to people who live in countries where Facebook is blocked.

Target Audience

It is targetted to people in Hong Kong and/or mainland China who need a professional photographer to assist them in taking photos for special occassions eg. funerals, weddings, family portraits, etc.

Objectives

We want to be able to construct a website that can attract more potential Hong Kong or mainland customers who need photos taken. Using the internet, we can attract more viewers and people on google who search for "photography in Hong Kong" will be able to find and access this website while also being able to browse through the many pictures and hopefully hire the photographer to take pictures for them. Since there was no photography site prior to the one we are creating for our client, we must work entirely from scratch and develop a website with our skills and knowledge on website coding.

How can I plan a website? What is the design process for creating websites? What are wireframes, storyboards and prototypes?
  • You can plan a website by using flowcharts, wireframes and storyboards to get the basic feel of your website.
  • For the designing process, you create a flowchart for the amount of pages that you want to make, then you create a wireframe to plot out where you would like things to go. Storyboards are used to flesh out the wireframe and is usually the same as your finished product. You use the storyboard as a base for your website so that you know how things will look before your website is complete.

Goals

  • Come up with a URL for the website
  • Choose a common theme for the website to be used on other social media platforms
  • Find photos that are able to be displayed in public.
  • Create a storyboard

Evidence of meeting with the client

Since the client is a close relative of Helen's, Helen was able to talk to him and ask him about the website over the winter break. We found out that unfortunately, most, if not all of his previous clients did not give permission to the photographer to showcase their photos to the public.

Subpages

Subpages on the website:
  • Gallery - slideshow of example pictures
  • About us - brief description/history of the company
  • Contacts - contact information including the address, email, phone number, etc.
  • Other information - FAQ, miscellaneous information

As there are four people in our group and four subpages for our website, we decided to give each person in the team a page to work on. The page that I was required to work on was the gallery page. Jaliff was the one who created the template for the subpages.

Tasks:
  • Our purpose is to open up more media information for the client's photography website. We want to give people opportunities to have more ways to contact and obtain more information about the client's website. Since this website will be catered to the client's needs, the website will be entirely customizable to fit the client's needs and hopefully make it easier for customers to browse and purchase his services.
  • Our target audience is towards people who live in the Hong Kong and mainland China area who have tasks that require a photographer's services for example, a wedding photo, funeral photo, etc.

Look and Feel

Our website has a sort of vintage feel to it. It feels rather old-fashioned to fit with the theme of the photos. While it may appear old-fashioned to a certain degree. The colors on the website are mostly dull in order to let the photography stand out more.

Template Wireframe

Our template wireframe was created by Jaliff and it was intended for the usage of all the subpages that our website needed. Sometimes the website seemed to be a bit restricting since there were times when I wanted to edit the template in order to properly accomodate the content that I was required to insert in order to complete the final product for my subpage.
