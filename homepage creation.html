Wireframe

Fireworks

Click here to view my Fireworks page.

Pros

  • The slice tool on Fireworks is convenient for inserting links.
  • I have more experience with Fireworks than Dreamweaver, so I am more familiar with Fireworks.
  • It's a lot easier to utilize the tools and make more shapes besides squares.
  • It's much easier to design and create things on fireworks as there are tons of different options and colors and shapes that are at your disposal that Dreamworks does not have.

Cons

  • You can't use html or css to code things. To design your website, you must use geometric shapes and tools, therefore you can't make a proper, functioning website on Fireworks.
  • It is ideal for making a wireframe and storyboard, but it is not the best choice for creating a real website.

Dreamweaver

Click here to view my Dreamweaver page.

Pros

  • You can see what effect you're having on your website while you're working without having to save and open it in your browser.
  • You can create a website without using actual code since Dreamweaver does the coding for you.
  • The design mode is useful to see how the final product will turn out to look like.

Cons

  • I'm not familiar with Dreamweaver since this is the first time I have used it.
  • The tools are somewhat difficult to understand and sometimes, I find myself unable to find certain things.
  • The shapes seem limited and there aren't as many choices as Fireworks.
  • It's very hard to insert pictures into Dreamweaver which I find extremely inconvenient.
